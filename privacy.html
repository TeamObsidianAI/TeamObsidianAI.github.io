Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Team Obsidian AI application ("the App") handles information in connection with its use of the TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share personal data from any users. The App is a personal desktop tool used exclusively by its developer to upload videos to a single TikTok account.

3. TikTok API Data

The App uses TikTok's Content Posting API solely to upload video files to the developer's own TikTok account. The App does not access, read, or store any TikTok user data, follower information, or account analytics beyond what is required for the video upload process.

4. Authentication

TikTok access tokens used for API authentication are stored locally on the developer's personal device and are never transmitted to or shared with any third parties.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

The App does not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are integrated into the App.

6. Data Retention

No user data is retained by the App. All video processing occurs locally on the developer's personal computer and is not uploaded to any external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect or process information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact
